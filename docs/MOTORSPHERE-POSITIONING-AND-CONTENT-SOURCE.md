## What MotorSphere Is

MotorSphere is a **multi-division digital automotive ecosystem** — not a classifieds website,
not a single-purpose marketplace.

The platform connects:
- Buyers and sellers of vehicles (private + dealer)
- Parts vendors, accessories suppliers and tyre fitment centres
- RMI-accredited and independent workshops, mechanics and panelbeaters
- Insurance, finance, warranty and protection partners
- Towing, roadside and emergency service providers
- Auction partner companies (strategic partnership model)
- Fleet operators and commercial vehicle businesses
- Advertising partners (automotive brands, insurers, finance houses)

MotorSphere is built specifically for South Africa:
- ZAR-native pricing (all prices stored and displayed in Rand)
- NaTIS process awareness (dealer verification, transfer support)
- POPIA-compliant data handling
- Province-level discovery and coverage

## The Auction Partnership Model — CRITICAL

**MotorSphere does NOT directly conduct, operate or administer vehicle auctions.**

MotorSphere's auction strategy is a **strategic partnership model** with established,
licensed auction companies. The division of responsibility is as follows:

### Auction partner's responsibilities (the auction company):
- Auction administration and event management
- Vehicle inspections, condition reports and valuations
- Bidder registration, KYC/FICA compliance
- Reserve price setting and floor operations
- Payment processing and settlement
- Vehicle transfer (NaTIS) and title documentation
- Customer support and dispute resolution
- All legal and regulatory obligations

### MotorSphere's role:
- Digital exposure across the MotorSphere platform
- Marketing support and content promotion
- National audience reach and traffic generation
- Lead generation and qualified buyer referrals
- Listing management and digital presentation
- Brand promotion within the ecosystem
- Analytics and performance reporting

### Content rules for auction copy:
- ✅ "MotorSphere connects buyers with established auction partners"
- ✅ "Our auction partner handles [list partner responsibilities]"
- ✅ "MotorSphere provides digital exposure and marketing support"
- ✅ "Auction partner listings coming soon"
- ❌ NEVER: "Live bidding on MotorSphere"
- ❌ NEVER: "Instant payments" (MotorSphere doesn't process auction payments)
- ❌ NEVER: "Reserve prices set by MotorSphere"
- ❌ NEVER: "MotorSphere runs auctions"

## Copy Rules — What To Write

### Supported claims:
- "South Africa's complete automotive ecosystem"
- "Connecting every part of the automotive industry"
- "One platform — every part of vehicle ownership"
- "Verified sellers — admin approved listings only"
- "POPIA-conscious data handling"
- "Province-level reach"
- "Compare vehicle insurance options"
- "Connect with insurance and finance partners"
- "Auction partner listings coming soon"
- "Built for South Africa — ZAR pricing, NaTIS processes, POPIA compliance"

### Unsupported claims — DO NOT USE:
- ❌ "Save up to X%" (insurance, any offer)
- ❌ "Best price guarantee"
- ❌ "Instant quotes" / "Instant payments" (for auction/insurance)
- ❌ "We are partnered with [named company]" unless confirmed in writing
- ❌ "Live bidding" or "Reserve prices" (implies MotorSphere operates auctions)
- ❌ "Just a marketplace" / "prototype" / "demo" / "seeded data" / "future backend"
- ❌ Any specific commission %, listing fee amounts or financial projections on public pages

### Language to remove:
- "just a marketplace" → "one digital ecosystem"
- "Vehicle Search" (category) → "Vehicles"
- "Vehicle Insurance" (category) → "Insurance & Finance"
- "Bid on vehicles" → "Connect with auction partners"
- "Live bidding goes live" → "Auction partner listings become available"

## Confidential Information — Do NOT Publish Publicly

The following content from the Investor & Strategic Partnership Proposal is **confidential**
and must NOT appear on any public-facing page:

- Specific commission percentages (auction or otherwise)
- Specific listing fee tiers and amounts
- Revenue projections, financial forecasts
- Investor returns or equity terms
- Named potential auction/insurance partner companies (unless formally confirmed)
- Salary or operational cost structures

Confidential content belongs in private investor proposals shared directly under NDA —
not on the public website.
